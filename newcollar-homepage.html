Electrician

$75K - $120K/year

Install, maintain, and repair electrical systems. High demand, excellent job security, and opportunities for specialization.

Plumber

$70K - $110K/year

Essential service that will always be needed. Work on residential, commercial, and industrial projects.

  • 4-year apprenticeship

HVAC Technician

$65K - $105K/year
